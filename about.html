About Charles

Charles is a consultant focused on improving businesses and the lives of those involved. Small and medium-sized organisations have worked with him to work smarter and better.

Tools and processes should help you work smarter, not harder.

With over 20 years of experience, Charles has helped businesses improve the way they work with simple and easy to implement ideas. He believes the best solutions are built at the intersection of business strategy, available technology, and user needs.

Things can get complex quickly; as such, a pragmatic, systematic, clear vision is essential to improve tools and processes that, after all, are meant to make life easier. Emotions also have a significant role to play, and implementing clear and simple solutions is of the utmost importance to create a pleasant environment in which everyone enjoys what they do. In the end, we're all suckers for solutions that just work.

Making sure the coffee machine never runs out of coffee

As a trilingual professional (English, Spanish and Portuguese) with over 20 years of experience, Charles' diverse skills and qualifications will make him an invaluable contributor to your business.

He has made his career in various roles and industries, in startups, small and big companies, including Apple and Google.

He's not only used to wearing many hats; he genuinely enjoys it. He thrives in an environment where no two days are the same, and he's passionate about continuous improvement in all areas, especially those that improve life and work quality.

In addition to being flexible and responsive, he's also a fanatic for fine detail and high quality. He believes in applying this same level of devotion to detail to all tasks, whether they are as visible as preparing materials for a top‐level presentation or as crucial as making sure the coffee machine never runs out of coffee. His mind continuously seeks improvement opportunities in everything he lays his eyes on, and once identified, he acts on it.

Charles will be thrilled at the possibility of collaborating so that your business and everyone involved continues to grow and improve.

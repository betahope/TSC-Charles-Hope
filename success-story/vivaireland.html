It isn't easy to imagine life pre-Internet before the web so closely threaded together individuals worldwide.

It takes work to stand out from the pack—work that VivaIreland is proud to have done. Founded in 2009, the company provides students and professionals with high-quality hospitality and accommodation to study, work and stay in Ireland. Even VivaIreland employees use their accommodation services when travelling.

"We want every customer to have a unique, memorable experience in a new city and a new house shared with new friends. At the same time, we aim to build a trustful relation with property owners by assuring them special care of their properties, just like they would!" explained Director Giorgio Gugliuzza.

Naturally, VivaIreland wants its customers to feel at ease in their accommodations and works hard behind the scenes to create a customer experience that is easy, intuitive and safe. To cultivate an inviting atmosphere, together with VivaIreland, we implemented a full suite of integrated tools and support channels.

Before our collaboration, VivaIreland used a few different systems for their daily work and support but felt there must be a better way to provide a more fluid customer experience, simplify daily tasks, and accelerate growth. They also needed a more efficient way to manage email and overall communications.

The collaboration generated big results:

  • We moved their booking system from being completely offline and human-dependant to being entirely online and automatic.
  • We designed a new and fresh website, entirely responsive and with best practices applied. The website became a source of valuable information for visitors and integrated with the booking system, and allowed stream communication with customers.
  • We implemented new tools and processes using Google Workspace and other tools to streamline some of the most common and inefficient daily tasks, saving VivaIreland time and resources that they now can dedicate to growing the business.

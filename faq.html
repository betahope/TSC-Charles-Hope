FAQ

I will be happy to answer your questions, and you will find some frequent ones below.

Why do systems matter more than goals?

Because the reality is that winners and losers all have the same goals. The systems they follow will define into which group they will fall:

  • All Olympic athletes want to win a medal in their competition. Unfortunately, only three will get one, and only one will be gold.
  • All teams want to win the championship. Unfortunately, only one will.
  • All entrepreneurs want to be successful. Unfortunately, less than 1% are.
  • And so on.

The key ingredient is the systems they follow.

Don't take my word for it. There are plenty of other insights on the topic, some of which can be found here.
